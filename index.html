About Us

A rehabilitation hospital built around you.

Hyderabad Care Center is a specialist physical rehabilitation hospital serving patients across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. We combine modern therapeutic technology with experienced, compassionate clinicians to help patients regain mobility, strength, and independence.

Whether you are recovering from surgery, rebuilding after a stroke, or managing a chronic orthopedic condition, our multi-disciplinary team creates a personalized recovery plan at every step.

Our Services

Comprehensive rehabilitation care

A full spectrum of physical rehabilitation services, delivered by specialist teams in a modern clinical setting.

🧠

Neurological Rehabilitation

Stroke recovery, spinal cord injury, Parkinson's, multiple sclerosis — targeted programs to restore function.

🦫

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Post-fracture, joint replacement, arthritis, and bone-health programs led by orthopedic physiotherapists.

🏃

Sports Injury Recovery

ACL tears, tendon injuries, muscle strains — return-to-sport protocols for amateur and elite athletes.

🩹

Post-Surgery Recovery

Structured recovery after knee, hip, shoulder, and spine surgery to reduce pain and restore range of motion.

👨‍⚕️

Pain Management

Chronic back pain, neck pain, and joint pain managed through physiotherapy, dry needling, and interventional care.

💨

Cardio-Pulmonary Rehab

Recovery programs for post-cardiac surgery, COPD, and long-COVID patients to rebuild endurance and lung capacity.

👦

Pediatric Rehabilitation

Developmental delays, cerebral palsy, and pediatric orthopedic conditions treated by child-focused therapists.

👩‍🦯

Geriatric Care

Fall prevention, mobility, balance, and strength programs to help elderly patients live independently.

🏥

Inpatient Rehabilitation

24/7 nursing, daily therapy sessions, and medical oversight for patients who need intensive recovery.
